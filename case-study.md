# Case Study: Document Data Engineering at Lawyers and Partners

## Introduction

Lawyers and Partners is a prestigious legal firm serving a diverse client base, primarily large multinationals in the
Financial Services sector, as well as a growing segment of start-ups within the same industry. The firm is poised to make
a transformative leap into digitalisation, with the aim of enhancing client services and developing innovative,
technology-driven offerings.

## Case Study Objective

The candidate for the Data Engineering role is asked to present an implementation plan and solution. This allows them to
demonstrate their ability to address the firm's immediate challenges and align with its long-term digitalisation goals.

## Scenario

Lawyers and Partners are currently facing several data-related challenges that are hindering its ability to fully realise
its digital transformation ambitions.

The firm's recruiters are overwhelmed by the volume of CVs received for various roles. To improve the efficiency of the
screening process, they have requested your department to create a solution that will ingest, preprocess and classify
documents.

## Process Specification

- **Ingestion**
  - Load documents in a dataset
  - Remove duplicates
- **Preprocessing**
  - Prepare documents for embedding/retrieval/analysis
- **Classification**
  - Use LLM to perform profile classification into one of the following relevant professional experience brackets: [0-2,
    3-5, 5-7, 7-10, 10+]

## Data

Use Kaggle dataset for resumes found on: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/snehaanbhawal/resume-dataset/data
